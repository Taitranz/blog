Valgo is a high-frequency forex visualization engine that generates annotated training datasets from market microstructure analysis. It delivers real-time tick processing with advanced SMC pattern detection (liquidity sweep identification, BOS/CHOCH recognition), enabling quantitative traders to label alpha-generating entry signals. The platform trains complementary classifiers: a signal generator and risk validator, using manually-annotated trades to refine model accuracy through iterative feedback loops. Built on event-driven architecture with tick-level persistence, Valgo provides the infrastructure for generating labeled datasets and optimizing autonomous trading systems.
